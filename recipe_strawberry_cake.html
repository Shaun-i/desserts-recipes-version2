Strawberry Cake

1 hour

  • Ingredients

  • 1 package of white cake mix
  • 1/2 package of frozen strawberries
  • 4 cups of confectioners' sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 4 of tablespoons milk

  • Directions

  • Preheat oven to 325 °F (165 °C).
  • Stir together cake mix and gelatin mix. Pour in water, oil, and eggs. Stir in half of the container of strawberries. Beat until blended.
  • Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Allow to cool.
  • To make the Strawberry Frosting: In a large bowl combine margarine, confectioners' sugar and the remaining half of the frozen strawberries. Beat until creamy. Add milk. Spread on cooled cake.
