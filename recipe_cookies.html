Cookies

35 minutes

  • Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

  • Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 °F (175 °C).
  • In a bowl, cream together the butter and white sugar until smooth. Stir in the vanilla and egg until blended. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; stir into the batter until moistened. Mix in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets.
  • Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, until the edges start to brown. Cool on cookie sheets for about 5 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
